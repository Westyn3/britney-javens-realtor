The Agent

Britney Javens

Britney brings a modern, energetic, and solutions-focused approach to real estate. With a strong background in client care, local market insight, and digital marketing, she plays a vital role in assisting buyers and sellers navigate the fast-moving landscape of North Atlanta.

Clients love working with Britney for her kindness, responsiveness, and unwavering commitment to making every experience smooth and stress-free. She is deeply invested in helping families — whether it's a luxury lake property, a North Georgia mountain retreat, or a residence closer to Atlanta's thriving communities.
