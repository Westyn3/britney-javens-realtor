The Selling Process

How We Get You
Top Dollar

Selling your home is one of the biggest financial decisions you'll make. Here's exactly how we make sure you come out ahead.

📊

Free Home Valuation

We start with a comprehensive comparative market analysis — pulling real data from recent sales in your neighborhood to determine the ideal listing price that maximizes your profit without leaving you sitting on the market.

✨

Pre-Listing Preparation

We'll walk through your home and create a personalized prep plan — from decluttering and staging tips to vendor recommendations for any touch-ups or repairs that will meaningfully boost your sale price.

📸

Professional Photography & Video

Every listing receives professional photography, drone aerials, and video walkthrough as standard. Your home will look stunning online — where over 95% of buyers begin their search.

📣

Aggressive Marketing

MLS listing, social media campaigns, email marketing to our buyer database, agent network outreach, and targeted digital advertising — we put your home in front of the right buyers fast.

💪

Expert Negotiation

When offers come in, you want an experienced negotiator in your corner. We evaluate every offer strategically — price, contingencies, closing timeline — to get you the strongest overall deal.

🎉

Smooth Close

From accepted offer to closing day, we manage every detail — inspections, repairs, appraisals, and paperwork — so you can focus on your next chapter.
