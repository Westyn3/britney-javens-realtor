The Process

How We Get You
Into Your Dream Home

From your first conversation to the moment we hand you the keys, here's exactly what working with Britney looks like.

01

Free Buyer Consultation

We start with a conversation — about your goals, timeline, budget, and must-haves. No pressure, just clarity. This sets the foundation for a focused, efficient home search.

02

Get Pre-Approved

Before you tour a single home, we'll connect you with one of our trusted local lenders to get pre-approved. In North Atlanta's competitive market, this is non-negotiable.

03

Curated Home Search

We'll set up a custom search based on your criteria and tour homes that truly match what you're looking for — saving you time and preventing decision fatigue.

04

Make a Winning Offer

When you find the one, we move fast and smart. We'll craft a competitive offer designed to win — whether that's price, terms, or timeline — without overpaying.

05

Due Diligence & Inspections

Once under contract, we navigate inspections, negotiate repairs, and make sure you know exactly what you're buying before you commit fully.

06

Close & Celebrate

We guide you through every step of closing — from the final walkthrough to signing day — so you can focus on the exciting part: moving into your new home.
